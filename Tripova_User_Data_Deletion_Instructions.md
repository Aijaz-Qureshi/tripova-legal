•	Email: [Your Email Address]
•	Phone: [Your Phone Number]

Upon receiving your request, we will initiate a verification process to confirm your identity. This may include:

Once your identity has been verified, our team will proceed with the deletion of your personal data from our system. Please note that this process may take up to 30 business days to complete.

## 4. Confirmation
Upon completion of the deletion process, we will notify you via email or phone call that your personal data has been successfully deleted.

•	Data that is required for legal compliance (e.g., tax records)
•	Data that is necessary to maintain the integrity of our services
•	Data that is shared with third-party service providers
